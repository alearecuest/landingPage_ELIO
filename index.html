Technology Stack

Powered by modern frontend-backend technologies

Elio runs on a clean, modular stack with Fastify, TypeScript, Gemini, and Angular. It follows a domain-driven architecture focused on maintainability, scalability, and clear separation of concerns. The backend is built to evolve fast without breaking core logic.

Angular

Robust frontend framework providing a seamless, responsive user experience for healthcare professionals.

Google Gemini

Advanced AI model providing intelligent natural language processing for accurate clinical note generation.

Fastify

High-performance web framework ensuring rapid response times and efficient request handling.

TypeScript

Type-safe development environment ensuring code reliability and maintainability at scale.

About ELIO

Project inspiration & team

During our collaboration with Asistencial Médica and guidance from our mentors, we discovered that physicians spend too much time manually writing clinical reports. We built ELIO to guide consultations with a streamlined workflow and AI‑assisted suggestions that generate standardized clinical notes, so clinicians can spend more time with patients. ELIO runs on tablets, phones and desktops and stores structured clinical information for future analysis.

About ELIO

Project inspiration & team

Inspiration: As clinicians and developers, we saw how much time is spent on documentation and how that reduces patient interaction. ELIO was born from that real need — to reduce administrative burden and improve clinical record quality. This is a portfolio project developed at Holberton School in collaboration with Asistencial Médica.
